## How Swasthya AI Uses Base44

Base44 was used to rapidly prototype the core product flow — the conversational onboarding experience and the connected-health-graph concept — before committing to the full Expo + FastAPI + Neo4j build. The prototype validated the central idea: that a multi-turn, memory-aware conversation feels fundamentally different from a stateless chatbot, even at the prototype stage.
