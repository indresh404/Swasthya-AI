# Doctor Dashboard — Features

The dashboard exists for one reason: let a doctor read a patient's *connected* health story in seconds, instead of reconstructing it from memory or a stack of paper. Every feature here pulls from the same graph the patient has been building.

---

**QR / ID Patient Lookup** — Scan a patient's QR (individual or family) or search by ID. The full graph-backed profile loads instantly — no waiting on the patient to recall or repeat anything.

**AI-Summarised Patient Profile** — Symptoms, how they've evolved, medicine adherence, and family risk factors — summarised from the graph, not a raw chat log the doctor has to read line by line.

**Doctor AI Assistant (Q&A)** — The doctor asks a direct question about a patient in plain language. The system answers from that patient's actual graph data. If the answer doesn't exist yet, the question is automatically queued and asked to the patient in their next check-in — the doctor is notified once it's answered.

**Family Risk Context** — Doctors see relevant, non-sensitive family health context (without exposing any family member's private record) — the same family graph traversal the patient sees, surfaced for clinical use.

**Medicine & Conflict Review** — Full visibility into a patient's active medicines and any drug conflict flags the OpenFDA checker raised — including the doctor's ability to see what was flagged before the patient even mentions it.

**Appointment Management** — Doctors see booked appointments with patient context attached, manage their schedule, and review who's coming in and why.
